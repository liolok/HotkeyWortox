[h2]Feature[/h2]
[list]
[*]Eat Soul
[*]Release Soul
[*]Store or Take Soul: Store into / take from Soul Jar, try to adjust the inventory bar Soul number to "ten short of overload limit".
[*]Soul Hop in Place: Jump at the position of character.
[*]Soul Hop to Object: Try to jump to the position of object under mouse cursor.
[*]Soul Hop to Furthest: Try to jump to the furthest position directed by the mouse cursor.
[/list]
